## About Anthropic

Anthropic's mission is to create reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems. We spend a lot of our time doing research to help further that mission, but we also work hard to build and maintain products that are useful and safe today. To that end, we're looking for talented and motivated people who want to help us grow responsibly.

---

## About the Role

This is a builder-focused position to lead startup adoption of Claude within the EMEA region. The role is described as a "0-to-1 opportunity" where the successful candidate will design programs enabling startups to access and develop with Claude while partnering with venture capital firms and accelerators.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and execute startup GTM strategy with tiered engagement programs
- Build partnerships with VCs and accelerators to drive portfolio engagement
- Create startup-focused offerings and pricing models
- Design Hex dashboards and SQL queries for analytics and ROI measurement
- Organize founder events, hackathons, and demo days
- Develop technical enablement content for startup builders
- Lead cross-functional coordination across Product, Engineering, Finance, and Sales
- Manage executive stakeholder relationships and communications
- Implement partner processes including deal registration and funding programs
- Gather startup feedback for product roadmap input

---

## Required Qualifications

- 7+ years in product management, founding, growth, or technical GTM roles
- SQL proficiency and Hex dashboard capability
- Deep understanding of startup ecosystem and AI/ML landscape
- VC relationship-building experience or accelerator partnership success
- Demonstrated 0-to-1 program building ability
- Complex cross-functional GTM program execution
- Exceptional communication and writing skills
- Bias toward action in ambiguous environments
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience

## Preferred Qualifications

- Startup founding or product leadership experience
- Direct venture capital or ecosystem background

- Existing VC and accelerator relationships

Active Partners
Partners with ≥1 API call in the last 30 days. A declining count signals portfolio churn before it shows in revenue.
Quick Ratio
(New + Resurrected + ExpansionChurned + Contraction)
Measures growth efficiency. How many units gained for every unit lost.
> 4× very healthy · 1–4× moderate · < 1× shrinking
Net API Churn
Churned + Contraction − Resurrected − ExpansionPrior Period Revenue
Negative = existing base is growing without new partners. The holy grail of net negative churn.
Gross Retention
Retained RevenuePrior Period Revenue×100
The floor — how much revenue survives without new business or expansion.
> 80% healthy · 60–80% watch · < 60% critical
Credit Payback
Cumulative RevenueCredits Granted
Above 1× = investment has paid back. The progress bar shows proximity to breakeven.
Token CAGR
(VlatestVfirst)12/n−1
Compound Annual Growth Rate of token consumption. Annualised from monthly volumes.
Last Active
Days since this partner last made an API call.
< 7d healthy · 7–14d at risk · > 14d dormant
Monthly Tokens
Total API tokens consumed in the most recent month. Reflects current scale of integration with Claude.
CMGR-3 — Compound Monthly Growth Rate
(VtVt−3)1/3−1
Growth rate over the trailing 3 months. Captures recent momentum.
CMGR-6
(VtVt−6)1/6−1
Trailing 6-month CMGR. Smooths short-term volatility. Compare with CMGR-3 to spot acceleration or deceleration.
CMGR-12
(VtVt−12)1/12−1
Long-term structural growth rate. If CMGR-3 < CMGR-12, growth is decelerating — investigate.
Absolute Monthly Growth
Tokensnow×CMGR-3
New tokens added per month. Rewards scale — a large partner growing slowly adds more than a tiny one growing fast.
Growth-Weighted Score
log10(Tokens)×CMGR-3×100
Balances scale and velocity. Log compresses volume so neither dimension dominates.
Projected 12-Month Run-Rate
Tokensnow×(1 + CMGR-3)12
Where this partner lands in a year if trailing 3-month growth continues. The primary power law ranking metric.
Revenue Impact (6-month)
Revnow×(1 + CMGR-3)6
Projected monthly revenue in 6 months. Factors in model mix since Opus generates more revenue per token than Haiku.
